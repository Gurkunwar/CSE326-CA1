CRISTIANO RONALDO

In our blog, the celebrity we are going to analyse is Cristiano Ronaldo.
Ronaldo grew up in Portugal as achild playing for local teams before becoming an
international superstar.Further into his success Ronaldo hasbecome one of the most
influential athletes in sport. This is shown throughout his global following which includes,
99.1 million on Twitter, 429 million in Instagram and 150 million on Facebook. With
almost 400 million followers, he is in an exclusive class of the most followed athletes.
But this comes with big responsibilities as being a positive role model to hundreds
of millions of passionate followers. So far Ronaldo has proven that he can balance being
a father to four children, a professional athlete and a positive role model to the world
through his social media. Cristiano Ronaldo is one of the most successful Soccer athletes
of all time with accolades of 5 Ballon d’Or’s (tied for most) and over 31 League titles
throughout the toughest competition around Europe. He is also the only athlete to win 4
European Golden Shoes. This is for the Soccer player who scores the most goals within a
season throughout Europe. With such a large following, businesses are always wanting the
opportunity to partner with Ronaldo. He is one of three athletes to be given a lifetime
deal with Nike (After Michael Jordan and Lebron James) worth 1 Billion dollars. It is
reported that Ronaldo earns 35 million pounds a year through endorsements and output on
social media. Companies which he has publicly shared his posts through his social media
include Nike, Herbalife, TAG Heuer, American Tourister, Egyptian Steel, PanzerGlass, and
Toyota. Like other athletes, Ronaldo had taken advantage of his platform and created his
own label selling a wide variety of products. These includes Cristiano Ronaldo Legacy
(Cologne), CR7 Blankets, CR7 Footwear and CR7 Luxury Underwear. These products can be seen
worn by Ronaldo in many of his social media posts with the link to purchase these products
in the bio. Lots of his products can be found online and now retail stores worldwide are
beginning to supply his products. In Australia, retail outlets have been selling the CR7
Luxury Underwear. Ronaldo also uses his platform to express his message and support to
serval causes. Most notably, he has partnered for foundations Save the Children, UNICEF and
World Vision. This is because he wants to help people less fortunate have opportunities to
become the best they can be and reduce poverty. This is close to Ronaldo as he was brought
up from humble beginnings and given opportunities from others who believed in him. This is
also shown publicly through his social media and making charitable donations in hope that
others will follow his footsteps and help reduce the cause. Cristiano Ronaldo is another
example to the world that regardless of upbringing, quality of life that anyone is able to
achieve their dreams and become a positive influence globally.
